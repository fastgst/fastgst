What is Tax Lookup API?

A simple, reliable API to search and retrieve updated HSN/SAC codes — built for integration into your billing, ERP, or commerce platforms.

CBIC & GST Council Sync

We strive to stay in sync with the latest CBIC and GST Council notifications to keep data fresh and relevant.

Vector-Powered Fuzzy Search

Built with AI-grade vector search, our API tries to approximate to the most relevant HSN/SAC codes even from imperfect keywords.

Full Developer Control

Built for developers in mind, Manage your keys, momnitor usage, quotas, and billing in Developer portal.

Structured GST Classification

Lookup for parent or child with organized Sections, Chapters, Headings, and Tariff items delivered in clean JSON.

Detailed Metadata

Access a rich set of metadata including GST rates, descriptions, cess applicability, and more ready for direct use in your apps.

GST Classification

Easily identify GST applicability — including Standard, Zero Rated, Exempt, RCM, Composition, and more. Perfect for building tax-aware systems with clarity and precision.

Cess Details

Capture cess breakdowns like Compensation Cess, NCCD, Health Cess, and more — right inside the API response. Helps in transparent tax calculations and reporting.
