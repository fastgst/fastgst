What is Tax Lookup API?

A fast, reliable API to retrive updated GST tax rates and resolve HSN/SAC codes — built for integration into your billing, ERP, or commerce platforms.

CBIC & GST Council Sync

We strive to stay in sync with the latest CBIC and GST Council notifications to keep data fresh and relevant.

Vector-Powered Fuzzy Search

Built with AI-grade vector search, our API tries to approximate to the most relevant HSN/SAC codes even from imperfect keywords.

Structured GST Classification

Lookup for parent or child with organized Sections, Chapters, Headings, and Tariff items delivered in clean JSON.

Detailed Metadata

Access a rich set of metadata including GST rates, descriptions, cess applicability, and more ready for direct use in your apps.
